About us

Jet-Marine was setup in 2017, extend from K.T. Lau Marine (Propulsion Consultency) in small boat design (below 5000tons）Propellers, as established since 1987, and partner with world class Manufacturer - Stone Marine and Mencast Engineering.

With 45 Years experience in Marine Engineering, and 35 years in fixed pitch, Custom High Performnce Quality Propellers, we had a Simple Singularity Linear Solution(1984), into delivering our expertise with proven results.

Through our experience, in response to our end-user toughest profile operational challenges, vital needs and requirements, we had our new Linear Database Program - onto custom our New Innovation Generation Optimzation Performance Propellers.
